Business Unlimited

An unrelenting stream of facts and information augmenting the Indian business and economy, Business Unlimited punches a pack with its elaborate articles. Indian economy is vast and widespread with its branches ramifying into Government emulation, Corporate aces and Core sector industries. Business Unlimited projects an analytical and deeply invigorating picture of Indian economy setting India as a lucrative investment land. Business Unlimited dictates an the interactive story behind the challenges and success of business portfolio of India. Through the bi-monthly magazine and digital space, Business Unlimited highlights policy makers, firms and enterprises crowning the decisive positions at various organisational levels in Government and Non- Governmental Institutions curating an illustrative picture of Indian Economy.

TARGET AUDIENCE

With a content that stirs up conversations on all platforms, while our voice is the one that matters to decision and policy makers, Business Unlimited finds its ardent readership amongst decision makers, consulting companies, law firms, political and bureaucratic officials, business and trade associations, chambers of commerce, students of business schools as well as global institutions like the European Commission, Ministries, state governments, Investment Promotion authorities of various regions/countries.

DISTRIBUTION :

We are developing a wide distribution network to have a good display on news stands or kiosks across the globe, especially at airports, railway terminals, key business hubs as well as upmarket residential areas. We also aim to be present in some leading hotels and also reach out to all the leading chambers of commerce, embassies and consulates and top companies in India and abroad, targeting our business and elite readership.

  • The magazine has an ample distribution in India, although it is institutional distribution.
  • The magazines are sent out to over 100 leading Foreign Embassies in India and also to over 200 Government Bureaucrats and Ministers in the Ministry.
  • All the major business events related to India, whether taking place in India or overseas.
